    Adventures in Ensime

    A blog like account of my time trying to get Ensime to work, as a reliable substitute for the hell that is Scala development in Eclipse.

    Setting up ensime

    1. Download ensime
    2. Try to build it, no luck
    3. Download binary
    4. M-x ensime - no luck
    5. Modify server.bat with

    if "%ENSIME_JVM_ARGS%"=="" (set ENSIME_JVM_ARGS=-Xms512M -Xmx512M -XX:PermSize=256m -Xss1M -Dfile.encoding=UTF-8)
